About Us

Nav Chetna is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children and communities. Our mission is to provide free education, essential healthcare services, and opportunities for skill development. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education and basic health facilities to create a brighter future.

Our Work

Free Education

We run free schools and tuition programs for underprivileged children, ensuring access to quality education and books.

Healthcare Camps

We organize free medical camps providing basic checkups, vaccinations, and awareness about hygiene and nutrition.

Nutritious Meals

Regular food drives ensure no child goes to bed hungry, focusing on malnutrition prevention and healthy growth.

Skill Development

We train women and youth in small businesses and crafts, empowering them to be financially independent.

Our Team

Dedicated Volunteers

A group of passionate individuals who work tirelessly to bring smiles to children and families in need.

Community Leaders

We collaborate with local leaders to address unique community challenges and create sustainable solutions.
